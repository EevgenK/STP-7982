About the Game

Trail Boss BMX is an exhilarating physics-based BMX game where players tackle daring trails, execute mind-blowing tricks, and push their limits in a thrilling outdoor adventure. Master the mechanics of balance, control, and technique as you navigate through increasingly difficult courses filled with jumps, drops, and obstacles. With highly responsive controls and realistic physics, every move counts in your quest to become the ultimate BMX trail boss!

Ride through forests, urban landscapes, and extreme courses, mastering tricks like backflips and grinds. Customize your bike, compete for high scores, and push your limits in freestyle or structured challenge modes. Whether you're a casual rider or a BMX pro, Trail Boss BMX delivers high-flying action!
